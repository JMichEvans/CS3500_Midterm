More Info

Graduation by Kanye West was released on September 11, 2007 by Roco-A-Fella Records. As Kanye's third album some fans claim it to be Kanye's best.








Coloring Book by Chance the Rapper was released on May 13, 2016 independently. This is Chance's most popular and recent album.







News of the World by Queen was released on October 1977 by EMI Records. Featuring songs "We Will Rock You" and "We Are the Champions" some fans see this as Queen's best album.






Thriller by Michael Jackson was released on November 30, 1983 by CBS Records. Thriller is Michael Jackson's sixth album and released with the famous music video "Thriller".







KOD by J. Cole was released on April 20, 2018 by Dreamville Records. It is J. Cole's fith album and was nominated for "BET Hip Hop Award for Best Alvum of the Year".







Demon Days by Gorilaz was released on May 11, 2005 by Parlophone Records. All of Gorilaz's music videos are animated with the same drawn characters as shown on the album cover.






Good Kid, M.A.A.D City by Kendrick Lamar was released on October 22, 2012 by Aftermath Entertainment, Interscope Records and Top Dawg Entertainment. It was nominated for three album of the year awards and won two.






Astroworld by Travis Scott was released on August 3, 2018 by Cactus Jack Records, Epic Records and Grand Hustle Records. This album stands apart with it's constantly changing beat.






Curtain Call: The Hits was released on December 6, 2005 by Aftermath Entertainment, Shady Records, and Interscope Records. This is Eminem's first greatest hits album.







Smyle by Kyle was released on October 2, 2015 independently. This is Kyle's second album and inclueded many features from Chance the Rapper to Yuna.







Flower Boy by Tyler the Creator was released on July 21, 2017 by Columbia Records. This is Tyler's fourth album and was nominated for "Grammy Award for Best Rap Album". Most fan's claim this to be Tyler's best album.






Kids See Ghosts by Kid Cudi and Kanye West was released on June 8, 2018 by GOOD music. This dynamic duo between Kanye West and Kid Cudi presented a of songs that were unique to both artists.
